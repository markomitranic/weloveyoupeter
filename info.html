PETAR Z MARKOVIĆ is a visual artist, born in july of 1984, in Belgrade (Serbia). Works as a freelancer in the feilds of typography, photography and graphic design.

Peter has all kinds of different hobbies. For instance from time to time he likes to impersonate Raffaello Sanzio da Urbino. Also Petar is passionate about fado music. Since his childhood Peter is avid collector of various and rare editions of Orvel's book "1984". From 2010 he is the chairman of Markovic Entertainment
